My Autobiography


Why I choose BSIT?

I choose BSIT course because I have interest in coding and web development.
I want to understand what happens behind the screen, and I want to learn these things
not just for myself, but also to create solutions that can make people's lives easier and
help other people.


Technology is already everywhere in today's world, and I believe studying BSIT will
allow me to grow with it. This course also gives me the chance to show my creativity
in both design and problem solving.


Most importantly, I know this couse will open many opportunities for me to find work
in the future. With that, I'll be able to support my family and give back to them while
pursuing something I truly enjoy.
